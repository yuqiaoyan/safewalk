Welcome to Safewalk Alpha !

Safewalk is a mobile application for Android that helps you find safe walking routes. We provide data on the number of crimes that's been reported in the past 3 months along your walking routes.

We currently support San Francisco, Seattle, and Alameda County. Our data comes from public police records from the Open Data program.

Thank you to all our testers! Please e-mail us with your feedback so we can make this application great.
